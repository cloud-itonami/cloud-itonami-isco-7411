# Business Model: Independent Electrical Practice

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-7411`
- ISCO-08: `7411`
- Occupation: Building and Related Electricians
- Social impact: local-jobs, electrical-safety, housing-quality

## Customer

- homeowners
- small commercial tenants
- property managers

## Offer

- wiring diagnostics
- repair quoting
- installation testing
- safety-compliance reporting
- invoicing

## Revenue

- diagnostic call-out fee
- per-repair fee
- monthly practice platform fee

## Trust Controls

- no live-circuit work without governor gate and lockout verification
- test records are auditable, not editable
- quotes and invoices require sign-off
